About me

I am a fifth-year PhD. candidate at ICT, CAS under the supervision of Prof. Qi Guo, Xing Hu and Yunji Chen from 2020. Before that, I received the B.S degree from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in 2020. My research interests primarily lie in the areas of computer architecture, system security, deep learning systems and domain-specific accelerators. I pay particular attention to build secure and efficient NN computing system. Ensuring the achievement of both goals is critical and challenging, especially considering the growing popularity of LLMs today. Through my work, I have published papers in top-tier computer architecture/system conferences/journals including ASPLOS, DAC, TCAD and NeurIPS.
